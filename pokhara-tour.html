Tour of Pokhara

Overview

Discover the serene beauty of Pokhara, Nepal's adventure and tranquility hub. Nestled beside the stunning Phewa Lake and under the watchful gaze of the Annapurna range, this tour offers a mix of cultural exploration, natural wonders, and thrilling activities.

Highlights

  • Enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Phewa Lake, reflecting the Annapurna mountains.
  • Visit the World Peace Pagoda, offering panoramic views of the city and Himalayas.
  • Explore the mystical Davis Falls and the Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave.
  • Experience the adrenaline rush of paragliding over Phewa Lake.
  • Hike to Sarangkot for breathtaking sunrise views over the Annapurna range.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Pokhara

    Arrive at Pokhara Airport (PKR) and transfer to your hotel. Relax by Phewa Lake.

  2. Day 2: Pokhara Sightseeing

    Boat ride on Phewa Lake, visit World Peace Pagoda, Davis Falls, and Gupteshwor Cave.

  3. Day 3: Sarangkot Sunrise & Adventure Activities

    Early morning hike to Sarangkot for sunrise views. Optional: Paragliding, ziplining, or bungee jumping.

  4. Day 4: Departure

    Transfer to Pokhara Airport (PKR) for your departure flight.
