Tour of Kathmandu

Overview

Explore the enchanting city of Kathmandu, the cultural heart of Nepal. This tour takes you on a journey through ancient temples, bustling markets, and historical landmarks, offering a glimpse into the rich heritage and vibrant traditions of the Kathmandu Valley.

Highlights

  • Visit the sacred Pashupatinath Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Explore the Boudhanath Stupa, one of the largest spherical stupas in Nepal.
  • Wander through the Kathmandu Durbar Square, a historic palace complex.
  • Experience the vibrant atmosphere of Thamel, a popular tourist district.
  • Discover the hidden gems of the city with a knowledgeable local guide.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

    Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM) and transfer to your hotel. Free time to explore Thamel.

  2. Day 2: Kathmandu Sightseeing

    Visit Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, and Kathmandu Durbar Square.

  3. Day 3: Swayambhunath and Patan

    Explore Swayambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple) and the ancient city of Patan.

  4. Day 4: Departure

    Transfer to Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM) for your departure flight.
