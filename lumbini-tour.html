Tour of Lumbini

Overview

Embark on a spiritual journey to Lumbini, the birthplace of Siddhartha Gautama (the Buddha) and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This tour offers a profound experience of peace, tranquility, and enlightenment, exploring the sacred gardens and monasteries of this important pilgrimage destination.

Highlights

  • Visit the Maya Devi Temple, the birthplace of the Buddha.
  • Explore the Sacred Garden, a tranquil oasis with ancient ruins and ponds.
  • Wander through the Monastic Zone, home to monasteries from various countries.
  • Meditate under the Bodhi Tree, where the Buddha attained enlightenment.
  • Learn about the life and teachings of the Buddha from knowledgeable guides.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Lumbini

    Arrive at Gautam Buddha Airport (BWA) and transfer to your hotel. Evening exploration of the Lumbini area.

  2. Day 2: Lumbini Sacred Garden

    Visit the Maya Devi Temple, explore the Sacred Garden, and meditate under the Bodhi Tree.

  3. Day 3: Monastic Zone

    Explore the Eastern and Western Monastic Zones, visiting monasteries from different countries.

  4. Day 4: Tilaurakot (Optional) & Departure

    Optional visit to Tilaurakot, the ancient capital of the Shakya Kingdom. Transfer to Gautam Buddha Airport (BWA) for your departure flight.
