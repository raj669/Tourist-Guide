Tour of Bhaktapur

Overview

Step back in time in Bhaktapur, a well-preserved ancient city in the Kathmandu Valley and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This tour explores the architectural wonders, intricate wood carvings, and traditional pottery of this living museum.

Highlights

  • Wander through the Bhaktapur Durbar Square, a complex of palaces, temples, and courtyards.
  • Visit the Nyatapola Temple, a five-storied pagoda dedicated to Siddhilaxmi.
  • Explore the Dattatreya Square, known for its ancient wooden monastery.
  • Observe traditional pottery making in the Pottery Square.
  • Savor the local delicacy, "juju dhau" (king curd).

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Bhaktapur

    Arrive in Kathmandu and transfer to Bhaktapur. Explore Durbar Square in the evening.

  2. Day 2: Exploring Bhaktapur

    Visit Nyatapola Temple, Dattatreya Square, and Pottery Square.

  3. Day 3: Changu Narayan Temple (Optional)

    Optional visit to the ancient Changu Narayan Temple, another UNESCO World Heritage Site near Bhaktapur.

  4. Day 4: Departure

    Transfer to Kathmandu for your departure flight.
